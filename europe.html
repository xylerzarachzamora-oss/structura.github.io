Europe Structures

1. Eiffel Tower – A revolutionary iron lattice tower that changed modern structural design.

2. Millau Viaduct – One of the tallest bridges, known for its elegant cable-stayed design.

3. Channel Tunnel – An underwater tunnel connecting the UK and France, a major tunneling achievement.

4. Colosseum – An ancient Roman amphitheater showcasing early use of concrete and arches.

5. Tower Bridge – A movable bridge combining suspension and bascule engineering.
