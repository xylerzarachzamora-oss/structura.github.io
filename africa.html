Africa Structures

1. Aswan High Dam (Egypt) – Controls flooding of the Nile River and generates hydroelectric power.

2. Karnak Temple Complex (Egypt) – Massive ancient stone structures showing early engineering techniques.

3. Great Pyramid of Giza (Egypt) – One of the oldest and most precise large-scale constructions in history.

4. Table Mountain Cableway (South Africa) – Engineering system transporting people up steep terrain.

5. Moses Mabhida Stadium (South Africa) – Modern stadium with a large structural arch design.
