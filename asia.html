Asian Structures

1. Great Wall of China – A massive ancient defensive structure stretching thousands of kilometers, showcasing early large-scale construction techniques.

2. Burj Khalifa – The tallest building in the world, designed with advanced systems to resist wind and seismic forces.

3. Akashi Kaikyō Bridge – The longest suspension bridge span, engineered to withstand earthquakes and typhoons.

4. Three Gorges Dam – The largest hydroelectric dam, used for flood control and power generation.

5. Petronas Towers – Twin skyscrapers connected by a skybridge, using reinforced concrete for stability.
